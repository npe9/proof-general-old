News about Proof General

  • 19th June 2002

    We plan to release version 3.4 of Proof General in the next few weeks. This update will have several significant improvements (notably to the synchronization support for Coq), and also includes fixes and updates for recent versions of (X)Emacs and various proof assistants.
    Please, please, please do test the development release for us in the meantime and report any difficulties, to help make the next release of Proof General as robust as possible. Thanks!
