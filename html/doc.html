The PostScript files are recommended over the PDF.

Note that both manuals (in HTML and Info formats) are included in the download. When running Proof General the manual is available from the "Proof General" menu. It should also appear in the system Info pages.

You can discuss Proof General with other users and receive announcements by joining our mailing list.

References

Ideas for the future of Proof General are given here:

  • David Aspinall. Protocols for Interactive e-Proof. Draft version, see here.
  • David Aspinall. Proof General Kit (white paper). Draft version, see here.

A technology overview of Proof General is given here:

  • David Aspinall. Proof General: A Generic Tool for Proof Development. Tools and Algorithms for the Construction and Analysis of Systems, Proc TACAS 2000, LNCS 1785.
    Here are some slides used for this talk and some other presentations of Proof General.

Proof General supports Script Management as documented in:

  • Yves Bertot and Laurent Théry. A generic approach to building user interfaces for theorem provers. Journal of Symbolic Computation, 25(7), pp. 161-194, February 1998.

It has support for Proof by Pointing, as documented in:

  • Yves Bertot, Thomas Kleymann-Schreiber and Dilip Sequeira. Implementing Proof by Pointing without a Structure Editor. LFCS Technical Report ECS-LFCS-97-368. Also published as Rapport de recherche de l'INRIA Sophia Antipolis RR-3286
